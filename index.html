Collector’s Corner

Handling & Cleaning

Use a soft brush to dust dolls. Avoid direct contact with water or cleaning agents. Keep dolls away from strong sunlight to preserve colors.

Display & Storage

Use display cabinets or acrylic cases to prevent dust. Keep dolls upright with stands. Store in a cool, dry place away from direct light.

Preservation Tips

Rotate displayed dolls to prevent fading. Wrap unused dolls in acid-free tissue. Handle with clean hands or gloves to prevent oils from damaging delicate fabrics.
